Owner's Biography

Muhammad Murtaza grew up in Michigan, where he never felt like he fit in. After moving to Houston Texas, he was able to unleash his artistry in culinary arts. Under Muhammad Murtaza’s guidance, the University of Houston cafeteria has evolved into a haven for innovation and creativity. With a keen eye for trends, and a dedication to quality, he curated a menu that celebrates diversity and caters to every palate his refined taste has led him to popularity with his best selling novel, “Cafe Chronicles,” he has managed to capture the attention of The New York Times, writing for them and co-creating three books. His work has been witnessed in major television shows and podcasts. He continues journey, inviting all who enters the doors to partake in camaraderie, community, and culinary delights
